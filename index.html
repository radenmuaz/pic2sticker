NoBG - Free AI Background Remover (No Upload)

Remove image backgrounds instantly right in your browser. 100% private, free AI tool, no image uploads required. Fast, offline-capable, and secure.

How it Works

Your images are processed locally on your device using WebAssembly and ONNX Runtime. This means your photos never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

100% Private & Secure

Whether you're removing backgrounds from personal photos, confidential company assets, or ID cards, your images never leave your computer. There are no external servers logging your pictures, no databases storing your information, and no potential data leaks to worry about.
